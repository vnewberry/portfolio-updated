About me

A small introduction about my self

Vance Newberry

Web Developer & Mathematician from the Bluegrass state

My name is Vance Newberry. I was born and raised in Paducah, Kentucky. In July of 2020 I graduated from Vanderbilt University's Coding Bootcamp and I'm excited to begin a bright career as a software/web developer.

Before starting my education as a fullstack developer I worked as a brick and stone mason for 3 years and as an apprentice mason/laborer for 5 years prior to that. I have a Bachelors degree in Mathematics from Western Kentucky which I completed in May 2015.

I am confident that my unique backgrounds in both mathematics and construction will enable me to excel as a developer in many different ways.
